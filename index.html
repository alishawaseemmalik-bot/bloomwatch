About BloomWatch

BloomWatch is an interactive platform that tracks flower blooming patterns using maps and charts. It helps us understand the impact of climate change on nature and seasonal shifts.

How It Helps

  • 🌍 Environment: Tracks seasonal shifts due to climate change
  • 👩‍🌾 Farmers: Supports planning for planting and harvesting
  • 👩‍🎓 Students: Educational tool for climate awareness
  • 🐝 Biodiversity: Protects pollinators and ecosystems
